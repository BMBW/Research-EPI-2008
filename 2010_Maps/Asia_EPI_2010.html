Best performer in Asian region is Slovakia with an EPI score of 74.456, ranking 13th in the world and worst performer is Turkmenistan ranking 157th in the world with an EPI score of 38.395. Developed Asian countries such as Japan, Russia, China haven't been able to get a good EPI score.

When considering the South Asian region best performer is Nepal with an EPI score of 68.236 ranking 38th in the world. Sri Lanka is the third best performer in South Asia with an EPI score of 63.714 ranking 58th in the world.. Bhutan is the second best performer in South Asia, with a score of 68.019, ranking 40th in the world. Iran is the 4th best performer in the region with an EPI score of 59.964, ranking 78th in the world. India and Pakistan are the 5th and 6th best performers, with EPI scores close to 48, ranking 123rd and 125th in the world. Bangladesh is the worst performer with a score of 43.592 ranking 139th in the world. Since most of the countries in this region are developing countries, they are mid performers in Environmental Sustainability. In East Asia, best performer in EPI is Japan, with a score of 72.541, ranking 20th in the world. South Korea is the 2nd best performer in East Asia, with a score of 57.045, ranking 94th in the world. Other countries in East Asia, which are North Korea, Mongolia and China, are the worst performers in East Asia with EPI scores below 50, ranking 147th, 142nd and 121st in the world, performing as 5th, 4th and 3rd best performers in East Asia. Although China is a developed country, it hasn't been able to perform well in Environmental Sustainability.

In Asian Pacific region, best performer is Phillipines with an EPI score of 65.685, ranking 50 th in the world. 2nd best performer is Malaysia, with a score of 64.986. Thailand is the 3rd best performer in Asian Pacific region with a score of 62.155. Laos and Vietnam are the 4th and 5th best performers in Asian Pacific with EPI score close to 59 and ranking 80th and 85th in world rankings. Two worst performers in Asian Pacific region are Myanmar and Indonesia with EPI score of 51.299 and 44.614. In Asian Pacific region, almost all the countries are developing countries.They have obtained middle scores in EPI scorings.

In Central Asia, best performer is Kyrgyzstan, with a score of 59.676 ranking 79th in the world. 2nd best performer is Kazhakhstan with a score of 57.303, ranking 92nd in the world. Other countries in this region have ranks over 100, showing weakness in Environmental Sustainability. Tajikistan, Uzbekisthan and Turkmenistan are the worst performers of Central Asia, with scores of 51.287, 42.269 and 38.395 ranking 111th, 144th and 157th in the world. Central Asian region can be taken as the worst EPI performing region in Asia.

Slovakia which is the best performer in the Asian region is a North Asian country. 2 nd best performer in North Asia is Czech Republic with a score of 71.621, ranking 22nd in the world. 3rd 4th and 5th best performers in North Asia are Hungary, Romania and Belarus with scores of 69.114, 67.009 and 65.369 ranking 33rd, 45th and 53rd in EPI rankings. Other countries which are Poland, Bulgaria, Ukrane, Russia has scores between 60 and 65, ranking 63rd, 65th, 87th and 69th in EPI rankings. Ukrane is the worst performer in North Asian region, with an EPI score of 58.218.
