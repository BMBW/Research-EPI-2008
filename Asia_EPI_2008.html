Most of the Asian countries are mid ranked performers of the EPI score.Developed Asian countries such as Russia and Japan shows high EPI Scores above 80.

When considering South Asian countries, Sri Lanka has the greatest EPI score with a score of 79.526 with an overall rank of 50. Pakistan has the lowest EPI Score 58.668. Nepal has a score of 72.098 and India has a score of 60.275. Since these south asian countries are developing countries, they are mid ranked performers which have only few resources and income to keep the Environmental Sutainability. Table 4 shows the South Asian Rankings of EPI scores.

In East Asian countries, Japan which is a well developed country shows the highest performance with an EPI Score of 84.538 by ranking 21st in the whole world. China and Mongolia have almost similar EPI Scores which are 65.082 and 68.113 which is very low when comparing to Japan. Although China is a developed country, it shows a very low EPI Score ranking 105th in the world.

In Asian Pacific countries Malaysia has the greatest EPI Score 83.978 ranking 26th in the world and Mynmar has the lowest score 65.124 with the rank of 104 in the world. Thailand has a score of 79.151, Vietnam has a score of 73.912 and Philippines has a score of 77.937. Other Countries in this region has almost similar EPI scores close to 66.

In Central Asia Tajikisthan has the greatest EPI score 72.371 ranking 79th in the world and Kazakasthan has the lowest score 64.95, ranking 107th in the world. Other countries in this region has EPI Scores between 64 and 72 which shows that these countries acts in similar fashion in Environmental Sustainability.

When considering North Asia, Russia which has almost 80% of the land in this region performs highly with an EPI of 83.851 ranking 28th in the world showing a great maintainance in Environmental protection. The greatest EPI scorer in North Asia is Slovakia, with a score of 86.03 and ranking as 17th in the world. Most of the countries in this region have an EPI score above 70, which shows a high performane, showing that North Asian countries have a good ability and resources to keep the Environment healthy among Asian Countries.
