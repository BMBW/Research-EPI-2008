Environmental Performance Index

2008 EPI Scores

EPI means Environmental Performance Index.This measures how a country has performed towards the Environment Sustainability by giving EPI Scores

2008 EPI Score has been developed according to 6 policy categories.They are,

  1. Environmental Health
  2. Air Pollution(Effects on Ecosystems)
  3. Water(Effects on Nature)
  4. Biodiversity & Habitat
  5. Productive Natural Resources
  6. Climate Change
